What are the signs that my roof might need repair or replacement?

Common signs include missing or broken shingles, granules in your gutters, visible leaks or water stains on your ceiling, sagging sections of the roof, and unusually high energy bills. If you notice any of these, it's wise to get a professional inspection.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

The duration of a roof replacement depends on several factors, including the size and complexity of your roof, the materials chosen, and weather conditions. However, our efficient team at Ready Roofing AZ is known for quick turnarounds. As one of our testimonials highlights, a house with a one-car garage was completed in less than six hours. We strive to complete most residential projects within 1-3 days, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine.

What kind of roofing materials do you work with?

We work with a variety of high-quality roofing materials to suit different aesthetic preferences and functional requirements. Our expertise includes, but is not limited to, asphalt shingles, tile roofs (such as concrete and clay), and various flat roofing systems (like TPO or modified bitumen). We'll help you choose the best material for your specific needs and the demanding Arizona climate.

What is the lifespan of a new roof?

The lifespan of a new roof varies significantly based on the material. Asphalt shingles typically last 20-30 years, while tile roofs can last 50 years or more. Proper installation and regular maintenance, provided by companies like Ready Roofing AZ, can significantly extend the life of any roofing system.

Do you offer warranties on your roofing services?
